## Why one studio

Hand-offs are where good ideas go to die. A design team throws it to a dev team,
who throws it to a marketing team, and somewhere in the relay the original spark
gets dropped. We kept it under one roof so the person who shapes the idea is in
the room when it ships.

## What we actually do

- **Build** — websites, apps, AI features, the technical spine.
- **Create** — brand, identity, the content around the product.
- **Grow** — the bits that make people notice, trust, and choose you.

## How to write a post

Add a `.md` file to `/content/blog` with a title, date and excerpt in the
frontmatter, then write in plain markdown. It shows up here automatically.

Headings, **bold**, lists and [links](https://maggie.agency) all render with the
site's reading styles — clean, constrained, easy on the eyes.
